compute triangle
STAGE 1
1. Enter length of the sides of the triangle.
2. Calculate area of triangle.
3. Calculate volume of triangle.
4. Display result.

STAGE 2
1. Prompt user to enter the length of sides of the triangle.
2. Collect length of the sides of the triangle.
3. Store result of length of the sides of the triangle as 'length'.
4. Calculate area of triangle with:
		( Math.sqrt(3) / 4) * (length * length).
5. Collect result of area of triangle.
6. Store result of area of triangle as 'area'.
7. Calculate volume of triangle with:
		 area * length.
8. Collect result of volume of triangle.
9. Store result of volume of triangle as 'volume'.
10. Display result (Area of triangle is area, Volume of triangle is volume)
